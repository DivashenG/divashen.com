Why Waves & Code?

My diverse interests never quite fit into one box, so I decided to create my own. Welcome to Waves & Code, a weekly newsletter where we explore the worlds of software engineering, surfing, leathercraft, and everything in between.

I envision this to be a space in which I share my professional experiences as a Software Engineer; my athletic adventures as a mid-level surfer/snowboarder and my creative exploration into leathercraft. All of this with the intention of fostering a community of like-minded individuals who can learn from one another and embark on journeys of personal and professional growth together. Join us in creating, sharing, and learning through Waves & Code.

What to expect?

Every post, I share a blend of personal experiences, insights, and discoveries. The general content structure will be:

  • Feature Article: A random thought, musing, or discovery.
  • Dev Insights: Any interesting dev content or tools stumbled upon.
  • Adventures: Join me as I share recent adventures or surf experiences as I attempt to transition from an intermediate to the next Kelly Slater (with a touch of humour, of course).
  • Leathercraft Corner: I'll take you along on my newest journey into the world of leathercraft.
  • Favorites this Week: Quick bullet points sharing the highlights and discoveries from the past week.
